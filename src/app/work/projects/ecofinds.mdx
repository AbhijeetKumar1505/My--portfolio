EcoFinds – Empowering Sustainable Consumption
Overview:
EcoFinds is a second-hand marketplace platform designed to encourage sustainable consumption by promoting the reuse of products and minimizing waste.

Key Features:

1. User-friendly product listing and browsing

2. Sustainable-focused UI with eco-friendly themes

3. Location-based item discovery

Seller and buyer chat support (planned)

Tech Stack:
Python, Django, HTML, CSS, Jinja2, Bootstrap, SQLite

Impact:
Promotes environmental awareness and circular economy through technology. Encourages users to reduce carbon footprints by opting for second-hand goods.

See the project on [GitHub](https://github.com/AbhijeetKumar1505/EcoFinds).
